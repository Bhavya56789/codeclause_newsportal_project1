Why Congress has more at stake than BJP in 5 state polls

The onus is on the Congress to prove that it can still win the three Hindi heartland states of Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh, which are key to securing power at the Centre Billed as the semifinals before the grand final in 2024, assembly polls in Rajasthan, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Telangana and Mizoram will be held between November 7 and 30. Political observers view the polls covering 683 assembly and 83 Lok Sabha constituencies as a litmus test for the Narendra Modi-led Union government. However, a deeper look at the contests, taking into account the voting pattern across polls in the past 10 years, shows that it is the Congress rather than the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) that has more at stake in these assembly elections..
